Konnichiwa Typhoon

posted on February 20, 2025

    Konnichiwa Typhoon: Tokyo’s Indie Rock Storm & the most chill party band you've ever seen. Emerging from the heart of Tokyo’s vibrant music scene, Konnichiwa Typhoon is an indie rock band that has been making waves since its formation in late 2020. The band is led by Peter Jens, a Chicago-born singer-songwriter and guitarist, alongside guitarist Ryo Typhoon, bassist Tsubo Typhoon, and drummer Ito "Big Hisa" Typhoon.

    A unique sound with global influences, Konnichiwa Typhoon blends elements of indie, slacker rock, and funk, creating a sound that fuses urban cool with garage rock energy. Their music channels influences from Western alternative rock while maintaining a distinct Tokyo edge. Since their formation, they have built a reputation through energetic live performances in local venues and a steady stream of online releases. The band has released several singles, including "New Age”, “Relax!”, “Pursuit of Perfection”, and “Odori” among others. Their music is available on major streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube, where they have gained an international following. In August 2023, Konnichiwa Typhoon performed at 7th Floor Shibuya as part of the More Tokyo Music event, delivering a high-energy set that captivated audiences, as usual.     A New Chapter - Recently, the band welcomed Takeshi on keyboards, adding a fresh dimension to their evolving sound. With their genre-blending approach and passionate performances, Konnichiwa Typhoon continues to establish itself as a standout act in Tokyo’s indie music scene. Konnichiwa Typhoon is just getting started, and if their latest releases are any indication, they’re set to take their sound to even greater heights. Keep an eye on them—they’re a storm worth watching.
